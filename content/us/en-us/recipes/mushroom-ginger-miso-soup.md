# Mushroom Ginger Miso Soup

Traditional miso soup made from mushroom dashi, with the aromatic addition of fresh blended ginger.

![Mushroom Ginger Miso Soup](https://www.vitamix.com/content/dam/vitamix/home/recipes/q2-2025/MushroomGingerMisoSoup_470x449.jpg)

---

## Ingredients

**Dashi Stock:**

- 4 cups water
- 1 (10 g) kombu
- 1/2 ounce (14 g) dried shiitake mushrooms

**Miso Soup:**

- 4 Tablespoons (72 g) yellow miso paste
- 1 Tablespoon (8 g) fresh ginger root
- 2 green onions, trimmed
- 7 ounces (200 g) silken tofu, cubed
- 1 Tablespoon wakame

## Directions

1. Prepare kombu stock by bringing water to a boil, then remove kombu immediately.

2. Add dried shiitake mushrooms to the hot stock and steep for 15-20 minutes.

3. Remove mushrooms and reserve 1 cup of the stock. Blend the reserved stock with miso paste, fresh ginger, and white parts of green onions until smooth.

4. Reheat the remaining mushroom stock, then add the blended miso mixture, cubed tofu, and wakame.

5. Cover and keep warm until serving. Do not boil.

---

## Chef's Note

Never boil miso soup after adding the miso paste, as this will destroy the beneficial probiotic bacteria. Keep the soup warm but below boiling temperature to preserve these health benefits.
